Accommodations


The Oberoi Udaivillas
All our events will be taking place at the The Oberoi Udaivillas and that’s where you’ll be staying as well. We're very excited about the place and would love to tell you a little about it:

The Oberoi Udaivilas is a luxury destination for marriages situated in Udaipur on the bank of Lake Pichola. The destination offers spectacular views. It is a luxury resort and the best destination for a wedding as royal as one wants. They offer personalized attention to detail and have a loyal list of guests.

The Oberoi Villas
All our events will be taking place at the Anantara Kalutara and that’s where you’ll be staying as well. We're very excited about the place and would love to tell you a little about it:

The Anantara Kalutara is a Geoffrey Bawa property. Who is Geoffrey Bawa you ask? He was an iconic Sri Lankan architect known for this signature "Tropical Modernism" style, defined by sunlit, breezy structures that meld into their natural surroundings. At the Anantara Kalutara, that means a lagoon on one side of the hotel and an ocean on the other. The resort has beach access as well as 2 pools.

Travel


Visas
Good news! It's super easy to get a short stay visa for India. Simply fill out this form. You should receive an approval within 24-48 hours. Be sure to print that out and bring it with you!

Arriving in India
Fly into Maharana Pratap Airport is about 1.5 hours away by road. If you plan to arrive before the 16th or leave after the 19th, see our FAQ section for vetted transportation providers you can use to hire a car and driver. You can also hire them to travel around Udaipur, India.

If you're arriving on the 16th or 17th, or leaving for the airport on the 19th, we will be providing transportation to/from the airport.

Local SIM Cards
The resort has WiFi but if you would like to get a local SIM card, you can do so at the airport. The rates are very reasonable and they fit your phone with a sim right there so its fairly easy. We recommend this option if you plan to travel around India before or after the wedding.
